Biographies

Nick Chubb

Born in Cedartown, Georgia, Nick Chubb grew up as a star in both football and track. At the end of his high school career, he was ranked as a 4-star recruit, and he signed to the University of Georgia to play college football in June 2013. He has two siblings, Zach and Neidra Chubb. His parents, Lavelle and Henry, are very proud of their son's accomplishments. Nick enjoys long walks on the beach and a good chicken parm. His birthday is on December 27th, 1995, which he dislikes because it is so close to Christmas - he feels that this allows him less presents. He is an Athletic Training major. Nick is passionate about the legacy of UGA football and is ready to make the Dawgs great again!

Jacob Eason

Jacob Eason was born on November 17th, 1997 to Tony and Christine Eason. A true westerner, Jacob was born and raised in Lake Stevens, Washington. He attended Lake Stevens High School, and by the end of his career, he was ranked as a 5-star recruit. After visiting many colleges around the nation, Eason finally committed to UGA in July 2014. His father, Tony Eason, played football at the University of Notre Dame. Jacob enjoys knee boarding and reading Hemingway novels in his free time. He often says that if he did not play football, he would've pursued musical theater in hopes of becoming a Broadway star. Jacob is HOT and READY to transform UGA football and give it the boost it needs to restore our legacy to its former glory!
